Introduction

Hello, and welcome to the OS revolving around the theme of our team dedicated to promoting science, technology, and engineering principles for high school students in and outside of the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). Arctos 6135 is an FRC team based in Toronto, Ontario, and was founded in 2016. We are confirmed to be competing in the 2027 BioCore Season in FIRST Canopy after a 16-17 record in 2026 REBUILT, our best performance in the past few years, despite not attending the District Championships in Niagara Falls, Ontario in April 2026.
